Enter expected wastage (%) for each feed, given the feed-out method used. See table below.
Note — A diet formulation is based on quantities of feeds consumed, so wastage does not affect it. However wastage will increase the quantities of feeds that must be fed out.

Table: Feed wastage using different feed-out methods

Feed-out method Minimum Typical Maximum
In the dairy at milking 0% 1% 2%
In grazing paddock, on pasture 5% 15% 25%
In sacrifice paddock, fed on bare ground, in ring feeders, or under a fence line 5% 25% 35%
On semi-permanent feed-out facility incorporating a compacted surface and low-cost feed troughing 5% 10% 20%
On permanent feed pad incorporating a compacted surface and purpose-built feed troughing 2% 5% 10%
On permanent, fully-developed feed pad with concrete surfaces 0% 3% 5%
