Cost definitions:

Cost $/tn DM (per tonne of dry matter)

This enables you to compare the cost of feeds per tonne of dry matter, but not per unit energy and protein.

Cost Cents/MJ ME (per megajoule of metabolisable energy)

This enables you to compare the cost of feeds per megajoule of metabolisable energy.

Cost $/kg CP (per kilogram of crude protein)

This enables you to compare the cost of feeds per kilogram of crude protein.
