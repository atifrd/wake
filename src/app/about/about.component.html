About Dairy Feed Tools

Dairy Feed Tools is an online solution that has been developed to assist Australian dairy farmers in implementing their feeding plan for the coming months.

Dairy Feed tools features a large feed library containing comprehensive nutritional specifications allowing farmers and service providers to generate a feed budget and a feeding plan and run scenarios to explore impacts of change.
