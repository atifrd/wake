  • Storage Facility Capacity

For baled and bagged products, calculating the capacity of the storage facility is easy. Simply enter the maximum number of bales or bags the facility can hold and the weight of a typical bale or bag which will be stored.

For bulk feeds stored in stacks, bunkers, pits, stretchable bags, commodity shed bay, silos, bins and tanks, you need to accurately measure the dimensions of the facility in metres.

Silage storages are challenging to measure as most are irregular shapes. Take into account the taper on the top of the storage and slope of the sides and ends.
