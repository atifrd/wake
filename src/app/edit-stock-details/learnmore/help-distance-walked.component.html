Accurate average distance walked and terrain are important because they affect cows' daily energy requirements.

How far do cows walk per day?

  • Cows grazing a large farm (300 Ha) with centrally located dairy will on average walk 4000 metres to and from the dairy plus distance walked while grazing ~5000 metres. Total 9000 metres per day.
  • Cows grazing a medium sized farm (150 Ha) with centrally located dairy will on average walk 3000 metres to and from the dairy plus distance walked while grazing ~4000 metres. Total 7000 metres per day.
  • Cows grazing a smaller sized farm (75 Ha) with centrally located dairy will on average walk 2000 metres to and from the dairy plus distance walked while grazing ~3000 metres. Total 5000 metres per day.

Source: Rumen8 Training Manual & User Guide, 2015
